In this key donjon you can open doors with keys. If you have a key in your inventory the door will open if you step on the door.
The key can only be used for one door.
Start at (1/99) and reach the goal at (95/5).

This level consists of the following new tiles:
K: Key, traversable, weight: 1, interactable, usable: 1
L: Keydoor, not traversable without key, weight: 1
