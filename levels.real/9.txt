In this level you have to push all buttons in the buildings which have a size of exactly 19 fields.
A building is enclosed of wall elements and must have a door.
The fields contained by the wall, excluding the wall and door itself should amount to 19 fields including the button.
After you pressed the last button you are finished and you can send the end command to clear the level.

This level has one new tile:
B: Button, traversable, weight: 1, interactable, usable: 1
